Music, Dance & Theatre

Students at Mount Zion International School are always encouraged to explore their potential in the world of Arts. The students are trained to play various musical instruments and are also taught Vocal Music.

A special emphasis is laid on Art and Craft work and it is an integral part of the school curriculum. Students are taught to draw, paint and create unique craft work using different mediums, materials and techniques.

Leadership & Participation

Our leadership programmes are rooted in a strong commitment to support students in their ongoing development as leaders. We inculcate leadership qualities in the students, by allowing them to take the lead in various activities and projects. We have various Leaders, such as Class leaders, Uniform leaders, House Leaders, and school volunteers, which are led by students and provide them with an initial grounding in leading others. We invite speakers from other parts of India and abroad for student workshops to develop leadership skills among students.

Our leadership activities help students to:

Become more self-aware

Boost self-esteem and morale
Practice public confidence and expressing themselves clearly to others
