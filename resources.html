Resources

Mount Zion International School Library features an extensive collection of Print and Digital resources to support and extend students’ learning. The library provides dedicated library space with age-appropriate reading materials and furniture.

We work very closely with students and teachers to ensure that the varied collections are suitable for the courses on offer. Huge number of reading materials are available for all subjects. Collection of National & International Journals and Magazines are available to keep students abreast of the latest findings and research. The resources are constantly being expanded every semester.

Technology is an integral part of the school; Multimedia and Audio & Video Resource centers provide students with additional resources to support their learning and research.

Resource & Program

Community Service

The School believes that true learning for life is attained through experiential learning. Community service forms an integral part of holistic education at Mount Zion International School. The school encourages students to implement meaningful activities that go a long way in building their character. The school organized Kids carnival and raise money for the underprivileged. The students have always responded with a helping hand to people across India affected by natural calamities like earthquakes, tsunamis and floods. The school has, over the years, visited and helped orphanages, old age homes, etc.

These opportunities enable them to be responsible leaders of tomorrow. The students are actively engage in enhancing the well-being of the local community and grow to become independent, pro-active learners. They are equipped and empowered to serve.

Experiential Learning

Mount Zion International School recognizes that the existing rote learning and chalk and talk methodology needs urgent up gradation in the present day scenario. Hence the school focuses on the concept of experiential learning that engages students in critical thinking, problem solving and decision making in contexts that are personally relevant to them. It inculcates all of these through an interdisciplinary program that is hands on and application skills oriented. This approach to learning involves action, reflection, conceptualization and reapplication.

The school is equipped with modern experiential learning labs and children are exposed to a truly engaging and interesting program through hands on projects which are a blend of Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics and Digital Media. Imbibing Life and Leadership skills is an integral part of the school program.

The School Program

Mount Zion International School believes in the philosophy of all round development of the children by training all faculties of the mind and body through round the clock engagement. Towards this end the school has designed an extensive academic and interactive programs to challenge the children to reach their optimum potential. The school emphasizes on developing the overall personality and character of the children through an inclusive program that encourages them to participate in a wide range of co –curricular activities, games and sports.

Mount Zion is committed to the development of the whole child and is founded on Christian beliefs and values whilst respecting the rich and diverse communities within the school and in its unique location.
